Blackbeard Prospect is an offshore drilling platform operated by Northstar Offshore Petroleum & Energy.

Overview

Blackbeard Prospect is a former British Petroleum offshore drilling platform acquired by Northstar Offshore Petroleum & Energy in 2005. Originally commissioned for deepwater operations in the Gulf of Mexico, the platform was integrated into Northstar’s fleet as part of the company’s early expansion into offshore drilling markets.

Following the Deepwater Horizon disaster, Blackbeard Prospect played a role in Northstar’s public repositioning efforts, frequently featured in company materials highlighting safety protocols and operational reliability.

As the oldest active vessel in Northstar’s fleet, Blackbeard Prospect has undergone multiple retrofits and structural upgrades to extend its operational lifespan. It is currently deployed in the Gulf of America, where it continues to support oil drilling operations under Northstar management.
