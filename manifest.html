Former employees and independent observers have alleged inconsistencies in publicly available crew records across multiple Northstar platforms.

Reported discrepancies include:

  • Personnel reassignment without proper documented transfer log or orders
  • Discrepancies in crew counts following reported incidents
  • Personnel records containing unexplained omissions and documented alterations
Northstar has stated that all staffing changes are conducted in accordance with internal operational requirements and international labor regulations.
