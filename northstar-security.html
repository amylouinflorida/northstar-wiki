NorthStar Security, LLC is a private security contractor frequently identified in proximity to Northstar Offshore Petroleum & Energy operations, particularly in regions with elevated security risks. While no formal corporate relationship has been publicly confirmed, the company has been described in industry reporting as an “adjacent” entity providing protective and logistical security services to offshore and inland infrastructure.

Independent journalists and regional watchdog organizations have raised concerns regarding NorthStar Security’s activities in several developing regions, citing reports of aggressive enforcement practices during the establishment and protection of Northstar-linked operational zones.

One frequently referenced incident, reported in local media sources, alleges that NorthStar Security personnel were involved in the forced removal of civilians from a coastal development area to facilitate infrastructure expansion. Witness accounts described the use of intimidation tactics and physical coercion, though details remain inconsistent and difficult to verify.

Additional allegations include:

  • Excessive force during checkpoint enforcement
  • Detainment without formal charges
  • Coordination with local authorities

NorthStar Security has denied all allegations of misconduct, stating that its personnel operate within the bounds of local law and international security standards.

Northstar Offshore Petroleum & Energy has not publicly commented on the nature of its relationship, if any, with NorthStar Security, LLC.
