Independent maritime tracking groups have reported irregular routing patterns associated with vessels operating under Northstar contracts. These observations include temporary transponder signal loss, unscheduled stops in non-designated maritime zones, and deviations from publicly filed routes.

Northstar has attributed these anomalies to routine operational adjustments, including weather avoidance, security protocols, and standard logistical flexibility required in offshore environments.
