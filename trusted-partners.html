The company’s Trusted Partner (TP) program has been a recurring subject of speculation. Critics have pointed to the limited public records of certain TP-designated entities, as well as their repeated proximity to Northstar operations without clearly documented commercial roles.

Some theories suggest that these organizations function as intermediary or shell companies used to conduct operations in regions with limited regulatory oversight.

Northstar has denied these claims, stating that all Trusted Partners operate under established compliance frameworks and contractual oversight.

Known or Suspected "Trusted Partners" of Northstar Offshore Petroleum & Energy
  • Chernorussian Offshore Petroleum & Energy (C.O.P.E.)
  • Emerald Isle Petroleum & Gas
  • Kellogg Global Transportation, Inc.
  • NorthStar Security, LLC
